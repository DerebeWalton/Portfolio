The End Is Upon Us Game


Kiernan O'Neil and I created this RPG that puts you into your mind, allowing you to explore a few of your emotions. The goal of the game is to escape.

The controls are spacebar to proceed and the 1, 2, and 3 keys to select your dialogue choice.
